Common Questions

Do I need to be a customer to apply?

No, but we heavily favour applicants who have used the kit or have a clear audience of developers who build production systems. If you have not bought it yet, we provide free access after approval so you can promote it honestly.

How are commissions paid?

Via Gumroad’s built-in affiliate system. When someone buys through your link, the commission is credited to your Gumroad balance automatically. You can withdraw to your bank account or PayPal from your Gumroad dashboard.

What is the 90-day cookie window?

If someone clicks your link today but buys 60 days from now, you still get the commission. Gumroad tracks the referral for 90 days from the first click.

Can I promote on multiple platforms?

Absolutely. Your affiliate link works everywhere. Many of our top affiliates share across YouTube, a newsletter, Twitter, and their GitHub README simultaneously.

How is the Partner Program different from the Affiliate Program?

Affiliates use a link and earn commission on individual sales. Partners (bootcamps, companies, communities) get bulk/discounted licensing, co-branded cohort options, and a negotiated revenue share. Partners are onboarded with a call rather than just a link.
