=========================================================
Created: 2026
Based on:

  - Full audit of the existing tensectra.com codebase (index.html, infrastructure.html,
    courses.html, cohorts, pro, consultancy pages)
  - KitImageNInfoPrompt.txt, EbookInfos.txt, BuyerOnboarding.txt, SellerOperationsGuide.txt

------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 1 — BRUTAL HONEST AUDIT OF THE CURRENT SITE
------------------------------------------------------------------------

What the current homepage does wrong:

  1. NO CLEAR IDENTITY IN THE HERO
     The current hero headline is not memorable or distinct.
     There is no single sentence that answers "what IS Tensectra?"
     A developer who lands cold cannot tell if this is a course platform,
     a consulting firm, a SaaS product, or a template seller.
     It is actually all four. That needs one sentence to resolve it.

  2. PRICING APPEARS BEFORE TRUST IS ESTABLISHED
     The "Available Now — $299" badge is visible on the Infrastructure Kit
     card in the path cards section — which is among the FIRST things a
     visitor sees after the hero. The developer's feedback is accurate.
     Price before value is a conversion killer, especially at $299.

  3. THE KIT IS BURIED AS A CARD AMONG THREE EQUAL OPTIONS
     The TensectraKit is the highest-signal, most tangible product
     Tensectra has — production code, GitHub access, documented patterns,
     Gumroad purchase flow, two SKUs (solo + team). It should headline
     the infrastructure page AND get a dedicated preview moment on the
     homepage. Currently it sits as one of three equal cards.

  4. THE INFRASTRUCTURE PAGE DOES NOT SHOW THE KIT IMAGES
     You have real product images:
       images/Kit/tensectrakit-cover.png.png       (solo cover, dark navy, cyan glow)
       images/Kit/tensectrakit-team-cover.png.png  (team cover, green glow, 5 avatars)
       images/Kit/tensectrakit-thumb.png.png
       images/Kit/tensectrakit-team-thumb.png.png
     None of these appear on the infrastructure page. A product page with
     no product image is a fatal trust gap. Gumroad pages show images.
     Your site should too.

  5. NO SOLO vs TEAM SKU SPLIT ON THE INFRASTRUCTURE PAGE
     There are two Gumroad products:
       gumroad.com/l/tensectrakit-core-solo   (1 developer license)
       gumroad.com/l/tensectrakit-core-team   (5 developer licenses)
     The current page shows one "Get the Scaffold" CTA with a modal that
     says "Email us to purchase." That is a friction wall, not a purchase
     flow. Visitors should see both options and click directly to Gumroad.

  6. "EMAIL US TO PURCHASE — WE RESPOND WITHIN 4 HOURS" IS A CONVERSION KILLER
     In 2026, no one waits 4 hours to buy a developer tool. Gumroad is
     already set up. The buy button should go directly to Gumroad.
     No modal. No email. No waiting.

  7. THE COHORT IS NOT CONNECTED TO THE KIT AS A NARRATIVE
     The current site presents:
       - Kit (buy the code)
       - Cohorts (learn in 6 weeks)
       - Ebooks (self-paced)
       - Pro (membership)
     as four separate products. The actual story is a single flywheel:
       Buy the Kit → understand the patterns → join a cohort → become an architect
     That flywheel is invisible on the current site.

  8. NO DISCORD COMMUNITY MENTION ANYWHERE ON THE SITE
     The BuyerOnboarding guide confirms there is a TensectraKit community
     channel. The ebooks confirm community access. The homepage and
     infrastructure page have zero mention of the Discord community.
     Community is one of the strongest conversion signals for developer tools.

  9. NO AFFILIATE / PARTNER PROGRAM MENTION
     This is a ready flywheel. Developers who buy the kit and use it in
     production are the best salespeople. There is no "Refer a developer"
     or "Partner with us" entry point anywhere on the site.

  10. THE "COMING NEXT" ROADMAP SECTION UNDERMINES THE CURRENT PRODUCT
      Showing "Pro Kit — $899 — In Development" and "Enterprise — Coming Soon"
      directly below the current $299 product tells the visitor "wait for the
      better version." Remove it or replace it with social proof.

PART 10 — BRUTALLY HONEST SUMMARY
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  THE REAL PROBLEM:
  Tensectra has a strong product, a clear architecture firm identity,
  real production credentials (fintech, banks across Africa), and genuine
  IP (the Kit, the cohorts, the ebooks). None of that is the problem.

  The problem is that the website presents all of this as a menu of
  equal-weight options and lets the visitor decide what Tensectra is.
  They cannot decide, so they leave.

  THE FIX IS NOT REDESIGN — IT IS NARRATIVE:
  The site needs one story told in one direction:
    1. This is who we are: architects who build in production.
    2. This is what we made: a scaffold from that production work.
    3. This is how you get it: buy it, or learn it in a cohort.
    4. This is what you become: an engineer who designs systems.

  Everything else (ebooks, Pro, consultancy) is downstream of that story.
  Once a visitor understands the story, they find their entry point.
  Right now they encounter the menu before they understand the restaurant.

  THE KIT IS THE FRONT DOOR:
  Railway's front door is "deploy anything without the complexity."
  Tensectra's front door should be the Kit:
    "The .NET 8 scaffold from production fintech. Running right now
     in banking systems across Africa. It is yours for $199."
  That is a specific, credible, immediate value statement.
  That is what should greet every visitor after the hero.

  EDUCATION IS DISTRIBUTION — ACT LIKE IT:
  The cohorts should be positioned as "want to understand exactly what
  is in the Kit and why every decision was made?" — not as a separate
  product. They are the Kit with a teacher. Make that connection explicit.
