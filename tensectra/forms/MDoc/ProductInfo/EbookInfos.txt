  Web apps cannot do this. Your app can.

✦ Push Notifications — expo-notifications,
  permission flow, token registration, foreground
  and background handlers. The full implementation,
  not the happy path.

✦ Offline Detection — NetInfo, OfflineBanner

  drops. Your app does not crash. It waits.

✦ EAS Build — eas.json configuration, preview and

  Expo Go QR link for iOS without a Mac. The
